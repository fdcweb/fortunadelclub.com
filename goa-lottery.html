About Goa State Lottery

Conducted by the Directorate of Goa State Lotteries under official government schedules. FortunaDelClub provides informational guidance and participation assistance based on publicly available information.

Weekly Lottery – Rajshree 10

The Rajshree 10 draw is conducted daily at 5:40 PM under the Goa State Lottery authority. Each day of the week carries its own draw name under the Rajshree 10 series.

Day-wise Draw Names

  • Monday – Rajshree 10 SOM Weekly
  • Tuesday – Rajshree 10 MANGAL Weekly
  • Wednesday – Rajshree 10 BUDH Weekly
  • Thursday – Rajshree 10 GURU Weekly
  • Friday – Rajshree 10 SHUKRA Weekly
  • Saturday – Rajshree 10 SHANI Weekly
  • Sunday – Rajshree 10 RAVI Weekly

Full Rajshree 10 Details →

Monthly Lottery – Rajshree 300

The Goa Monthly Lottery (Rajshree 300) is conducted on a monthly basis as per officially notified schedules issued by the Directorate of Goa State Lotteries.

Key Information

  • Conducted monthly as per government notification
  • Prize structure officially declared by authorities
  • Ticket series and numbers assigned per official convention
  • Results declared through official government channels
