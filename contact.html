Transparency & Disclaimer

FortunaDelClub is an independent lottery assistance and informational service. We provide guidance on official draw schedules, prize structures, participation procedures, and result verification for Goa and Kerala State Lotteries.

What We Are Not

  • Not a government authority or official lottery operator
  • Not affiliated with Goa State Lotteries or Kerala State Lotteries
  • Not a ticket seller or procurement agent
  • Not involved in conducting lottery draws
  • Do not guarantee winnings or predict results

Official Information

  • All lottery draws are conducted exclusively by government authorities
  • Prize declarations and results are official government announcements
  • Draw schedules may change based on official notifications
  • Participants are responsible for verifying all details through official channels
  • Visit Goa or Kerala official sites

Participants should ensure compliance with the laws applicable in their own jurisdiction. FortunaDelClub operates transparently and welcomes verification of our information against official sources.
