Kerala State Lottery

About Kerala State Lottery

Conducted by the Directorate of Kerala State Lotteries under the Government of Kerala, with weekly and bumper draws issued through official notifications.

Weekly Lottery (Monday–Sunday)

Kerala State Lottery conducts a named weekly draw on each day of the week. All draws are held at approximately 3:00 PM on their scheduled day.

Day-wise Draw Schedule

  • Monday – Bhagyathara
  • Tuesday – SthreeSakthi
  • Wednesday – Dhanalekshmi
  • Thursday – KarunyaPlus
  • Friday – SuvarnaKeralam
  • Saturday – Karunya
  • Sunday – Samrudhi

Full Weekly Details →

Bumper Lottery

Kerala State Lottery conducts bumper draws approximately once every two months on special occasions. Bumper draws are held at approximately 2:00 PM on the scheduled date.

Annual Bumper Schedule (Approx.)

  • January – Christmas New Year Bumper
  • March – Summer Bumper
  • May – Vishu Bumper
  • July – Monsoon Bumper
  • September – Thiruvonam Bumper
  • November – Pooja Bumper
