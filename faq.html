Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about FortunaDelClub and state lottery participation assistance.

What does FortunaDelClub do?
FortunaDelClub is an independent lottery assistance and informational service. We provide: guidance on official draw schedules, clarification of prize structures, assistance with participation procedures, and support with result verification. We do not conduct lottery draws and do not determine lottery results.
Is FortunaDelClub affiliated with the Government?
No. FortunaDelClub is not affiliated with any State Lottery Department or Government authority. All lottery draws, prize declarations, and official notifications are managed exclusively by the respective state lottery authorities — the Directorate of Goa State Lotteries and the Directorate of Kerala State Lotteries.
Does FortunaDelClub sell lottery tickets?
FortunaDelClub does not operate as a lottery draw authority. Participation assistance, where applicable, is facilitated strictly in accordance with officially notified procedures and applicable regulations. We encourage participants to rely on official government notifications for all final confirmations.
Are lottery results published by FortunaDelClub official?
No. Official lottery results are declared only by the respective State Lottery authorities. FortunaDelClub may share publicly available result information for reference purposes, but participants must verify results through official sources: Goa | Kerala.
Is participation in Goa and Kerala State Lottery legal?
Goa State Lottery and Kerala State Lottery are government-authorised lottery schemes conducted under their respective state regulations. Participants are responsible for ensuring compliance with the laws applicable in their own jurisdiction before participating.
How are draw schedules determined?
Draw schedules, prize structures, ticket series, and other details are officially notified by the respective State Lottery Departments. Schedules may change based on government notifications. Participants are advised to verify updates through official sources.
Can participants choose specific draw days or schemes?
Yes, participation interest can be expressed for specific officially notified draws. Final availability and procedures depend on officially announced draw structures and applicable rules.
Does ticket number format influence winning chances?
No. Ticket numbers are pre-printed and assigned according to officially notified numbering conventions. Winning numbers are determined solely by the respective State Lottery authorities through the official draw process.
How are prizes claimed?
Prize claim procedures are governed entirely by the respective State Lottery Departments. Participants must follow official guidelines for ticket validation, documentation, and claim submission. FortunaDelClub may provide general guidance regarding publicly available claim procedures.
Is the information on this website official?
The website provides general informational guidance only. Official lottery information, results, and rules are published by the respective state lottery departments. Goa: statelotteries.goa.gov.in | Kerala: statelottery.kerala.gov.in
