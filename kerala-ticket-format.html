Ticket Format Structure

Each Kerala State Lottery ticket carries a unique identification comprising a two-letter series code followed by a 6-digit number.

Format Breakdown

  • Two-Letter Series: Assigned per draw (e.g., AB, CD, KR)
  • 6-Digit Number: Unique number within the series (e.g., 123456)
  • Example: AB 123456
  • Series codes and numbers are pre-printed per official draw notification
  • Different series may carry different prize eligibility

Weekly Draws

  • Each weekly draw (Bhagyathara, SthreeSakthi, etc.) carries its own series prefix
  • Series assigned separately for each draw date

Bumper Draws

  • Bumper lottery tickets carry draw-specific series prefixes
  • Ticket series are part of the official bumper notification

How to Verify a Kerala Lottery Ticket

After the official draw, winners are identified by matching the exact ticket series code and 6-digit number against the declared results.

Verification Steps

  • Note the two-letter series and 6-digit number on your ticket
  • Visit the official Kerala State Lottery result page
  • Check the draw results for your specific draw date and draw name
  • Match your series and number against the winning list
  • If matched, follow official prize claim procedures

Prize Claim

  • Prize claim procedures are governed by the Directorate of Kerala State Lotteries
  • Participants must submit original winning tickets with valid identification
  • FortunaDelClub provides general guidance on the official claim process
