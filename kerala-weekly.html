Kerala Weekly Lottery – 7 Draws, 7 Days

Kerala State Lottery conducts a uniquely named draw every day of the week at 3:00 PM. FortunaDelClub provides participation assistance and informational guidance for all weekly draws.

About the Weekly Draws

🌟 Bhagyathara (Mon)

Monday's weekly draw under the Kerala State Lottery. Draw held at 3:00 PM. Results declared officially by the Directorate of Kerala State Lotteries.

💪 SthreeSakthi (Tue)

Tuesday's weekly draw. SthreeSakthi is one of Kerala's well-known lottery series, conducted under official government notification.

💰 Dhanalekshmi (Wed)

Wednesday's draw. Official prize structures and ticket series are announced by the Kerala State Lottery Directorate for each draw.

🙏 KarunyaPlus (Thu)

Thursday's weekly draw. KarunyaPlus is a popular draw under the Kerala State Lottery series, conducted at 3:00 PM officially.

✨ SuvarnaKeralam (Fri)

Friday's draw. Draws follow official schedules and prize structures notified by the Directorate of Kerala State Lotteries.

❤️ Karunya (Sat)

Saturday's weekly draw. Karunya is among Kerala's most recognised lottery names, conducted every Saturday at 3:00 PM.

🌈 Samrudhi (Sun)

Sunday's weekly draw. Samrudhi completes the 7-day Kerala State Lottery weekly cycle, officially conducted at 3:00 PM.
