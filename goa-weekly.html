About Rajshree 10

The Rajshree 10 is the flagship weekly lottery draw of the Goa State Lottery, conducted under the Directorate of Goa State Lotteries. It is among the most actively participated draws in the Goa State Lottery series.

Key Facts

  • Conducted daily, 7 days a week
  • Draw time: 5:40 PM (as officially notified)
  • Results declared via official channels
  • Prize structure as per official lottery notification
  • Live draw available on official YouTube channel

Ticket Number Format

Goa State Lottery tickets carry a structured number format. Understanding this helps participants verify their tickets against official draw results.

Format Overview

  • Tickets carry a series letter and a 6-digit number
  • Series letters are assigned per official draw batch
  • Numbers are pre-printed and assigned per official convention
  • Winning numbers are determined solely by the Goa State Lottery authority
