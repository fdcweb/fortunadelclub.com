Rajshree 300 – Monthly Draw Information

About Rajshree 300

The Rajshree 300 is the monthly lottery draw conducted by the Directorate of Goa State Lotteries. It is conducted as per officially issued government notifications and draw schedules.

Key Information

  • Conducted on a monthly basis per official notification
  • Draw date and time announced by the Goa State Lottery Directorate
  • Prize structure officially notified prior to each draw
  • Results declared exclusively through official government channels
  • Ticket series and structure governed by official conventions
