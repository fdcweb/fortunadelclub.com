# 11 — Content Update Guidelines

**Document:** Content Update Frequency and Guidelines
**Knowledge Base:** FortunaDelClub Website Strategy
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-25

---

## Why Content Updates Matter

Google rewards fresh, accurate content — especially in the gambling/betting niche where information changes frequently (bonus terms, platform features, regulations). Outdated content:
- Damages user trust
- Can create compliance issues (e.g., advertising bonuses that no longer exist)
- Loses search rankings to competitors with fresher content

---

## Update Frequency by Content Type

| Content Type | Minimum Update Frequency | Trigger for Immediate Update |
|-------------|--------------------------|------------------------------|
| Platform reviews | Every 6 months | Platform changes terms, bonuses, or licensing |
| Bonus pages | Every 3 months | Bonus offer changes or expires |
| How-to guides | Annually | Platform UI changes or new features |
| FAQ pages | Quarterly | New common questions emerge |
| Legal pages | Quarterly | Regulatory or business changes |
| News articles | N/A (evergreen after publish) | Factual errors discovered |
| Homepage | Monthly | Featured platforms or offers change |
| Responsible gambling page | Bi-annually | New resources or regulatory requirements |

---

## What to Check When Updating a Review

When updating a platform review, verify and update:

- [ ] Welcome bonus amount and terms (wagering requirements, expiry)
- [ ] Minimum deposit and withdrawal amounts
- [ ] Available payment methods (especially PIX availability)
- [ ] Licensing status (is the platform still licensed in Brazil?)
- [ ] Mobile app availability and quality
- [ ] Customer support channels and response times
- [ ] Any new features or significant changes
- [ ] Overall rating — adjust if platform quality has changed
- [ ] "Last verified" date at top of page

---

## Content Freshness Signals

Add these elements to signal content freshness to both users and Google:

1. **"Atualizado em [date]"** — Visible at the top of every review and guide
2. **"Verificado em [date]"** — For bonus and platform information
3. **Version notes** — For significant updates, add a brief note: "Atualizado em abril 2026: novo bonus de boas-vindas disponivel"

## Update Workflow

### Step 1: Identify What Needs Updating
- Check platform websites directly for current bonus terms
- Monitor regulatory news for Brazilian gambling law changes
- Review Google Search Console for pages losing traffic (may indicate outdated content)
- Check WhatsApp community for questions about outdated information

### Step 2: Make the Updates
- Update all factual information
- Update the "last verified/updated" date
- Add a brief update note if the change is significant
- Check and update any internal links that may be affected

### Step 3: Re-optimize if Needed
- If the page has lost rankings, consider refreshing the introduction and meta description
- Add new FAQ items if relevant questions have emerged
- Ensure the WhatsApp CTA is still present and functional

### Step 4: Republish
- Save and publish the updated page
- If the update is significant, consider sharing in the WhatsApp community

---

## Content Retirement Policy

Some content may need to be retired rather than updated:

- **Platform no longer available in Brazil** — Remove review or add prominent notice; redirect URL if possible
- **Bonus no longer available** — Update page to reflect current offers or remove if no replacement
- **Regulatory change makes content non-compliant** — Update immediately or unpublish

When retiring content:
- Set up a 301 redirect to the most relevant current page
- Do not leave 404 pages — always redirect
- Update any internal links pointing to the retired page

---

## Content Audit Schedule

Perform a full content audit:
- **Quarterly** — Check all bonus and review pages for accuracy
- **Bi-annually** — Full site audit including guides, FAQs, and legal pages
- **Annually** — Strategic review of all content against current SEO and business goals
