# 03 — Content Strategy

**Document:** Content Types and Strategy Framework
**Knowledge Base:** FortunaDelClub Website Strategy
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-25

---

## Content Philosophy

Every piece of content on FortunaDelClub must serve one or more of these purposes:
1. **Rank** — Target a specific keyword and satisfy search intent
2. **Educate** — Provide genuine value to the reader
3. **Convert** — Move the user toward a WhatsApp contact or platform visit
4. **Trust** — Reinforce the site's credibility and authority

Content that does not serve at least one of these purposes should not be published.

---

## Content Types

### 1. Platform Reviews
**Purpose:** Help users choose the right betting/casino platform
**Format:** Long-form review (1,500–3,000 words)
**Required elements:**
- Platform overview and licensing information
- Pros and cons
- Bonus and promotion details (with terms clearly explained)
- Payment methods (focus on PIX, Boleto)
- Mobile experience
- Customer support quality
- Overall rating/score
- Responsible gambling disclaimer
- WhatsApp CTA

### 2. How-To Guides and Educational Content
**Purpose:** Educate beginners and intermediate users
**Format:** Step-by-step guides (800–2,000 words)
**Topics include:**
- How to register on a betting platform
- How to use PIX for deposits/withdrawals
- How to understand odds formats
- How to claim and use bonuses
- How to bet on specific sports (football, basketball, etc.)

### 3. Strategy and Tips Content
**Purpose:** Provide value to experienced bettors
**Format:** Informational articles (1,000–2,500 words)
**Required elements:**
- Clear disclaimer that no strategy guarantees winnings
- Responsible gambling reminder
- Practical, actionable information
- No "guaranteed profit" language

### 4. Comparison Pages
**Purpose:** Help users compare platforms side-by-side
**Format:** Structured comparison tables + narrative (1,000–2,000 words)
**Examples:**
- "Bet365 vs Betano — Qual e Melhor para Brasileiros?"
- "Melhores Bonus de Boas-Vindas 2026"

### 5. FAQ Pages
**Purpose:** Capture long-tail search queries and provide quick answers
**Format:** Question-and-answer format (see 08-faq-strategy.md)

### 6. News and Updates
**Purpose:** Keep content fresh and capture trending searches
**Format:** Short news articles (300–600 words)
**Topics:** Regulatory updates, new platform launches, major promotions

### 7. Responsible Gambling Content
**Purpose:** Fulfill compliance obligations and build trust
**Format:** Dedicated pages + embedded sections
**Required:** At least one dedicated responsible gambling page on the site

---

## Content Hierarchy

Pillar Pages (Main Categories) contain Sub-pages (Specific Topics) which contain FAQ entries and related guides.

### Pillar Page Examples
- Apostas Esportivas (Sports Betting)
- Cassinos Online (Online Casinos)
- Bonus e Promocoes (Bonuses and Promotions)
- Jogo Responsavel (Responsible Gambling)

---

## Content Quality Standards

### Mandatory for Every Page
- Target keyword in title (H1)
- Target keyword in first paragraph
- Meta description written (150–160 characters)
- At least 2 internal links to related pages
- At least 1 WhatsApp CTA
- Responsible gambling disclaimer (where applicable)
- Last updated date visible

### Prohibited Content
- Guaranteed winning claims
- False bonus information
- Content targeting minors
- Misleading platform comparisons
- Unverified statistics or claims

---

## Content Calendar Principles

- **Reviews:** Update at minimum every 6 months or when platform terms change
- **Guides:** Review annually; update when regulations or platform features change
- **News:** Publish as events occur
- **FAQs:** Review quarterly; add new questions based on search data
