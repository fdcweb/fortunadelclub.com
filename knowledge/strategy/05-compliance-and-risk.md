# 05 — Compliance and Risk

**Document:** Compliance Rules, Mandatory Disclaimers, and Regulatory Sensitivity
**Knowledge Base:** FortunaDelClub Website Strategy
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-25

---

## Regulatory Context

FortunaDelClub operates in the Brazilian market, which is subject to:
- **Lei 14.790/2023** — Brazil's sports betting and online gambling regulation law
- **CONAR guidelines** — Brazilian advertising self-regulation
- **LGPD (Lei Geral de Protecao de Dados)** — Brazil's data protection law (equivalent to GDPR)
- **Consumer protection laws (CDC)** — Codigo de Defesa do Consumidor

The site is an informational/community platform, NOT a licensed gambling operator. This distinction is critical for compliance positioning.

---

## Mandatory Disclaimers

### 1. Site-Wide Footer Disclaimer
Every page must include a footer disclaimer stating:
- The site is for informational purposes only
- Gambling involves risk and is not suitable for everyone
- Users must be 18+ to gamble
- Affiliate relationship disclosure (if applicable)

**Example footer text:**
"FortunaDelClub e um site de informacao e comunidade. Nao operamos jogos de azar. Apostas envolvem risco — jogue com responsabilidade. Conteudo destinado a maiores de 18 anos. Podemos receber comissoes por indicacoes."

### 2. Review Page Disclaimer
All platform review pages must include:
- A note that terms and conditions may change
- A reminder to read the full T&Cs of any platform before registering
- Responsible gambling reminder

### 3. Strategy Content Disclaimer
All strategy/tips pages must include:
- "Nenhuma estrategia garante lucros. Apostas envolvem risco de perda financeira."
- Link to the responsible gambling page

### 4. Bonus Content Disclaimer
All bonus/promotion pages must include:
- "Bonus sujeitos a termos e condicoes. Leia os requisitos de apostas antes de aceitar qualquer oferta."
- Wagering requirement explanation where relevant

---

## Age Restriction

- All content is for adults 18+ only
- No content should appeal specifically to minors
- Age gate or disclaimer must be visible on the homepage
- No use of cartoon characters, youth-oriented imagery, or language targeting minors

---

## Responsible Gambling Requirements

### Mandatory Elements
- At least one dedicated "Jogo Responsavel" page on the site
- Links to responsible gambling resources (e.g., Jogadores Anonimos Brazil)
- Self-exclusion information for major platforms
- Problem gambling helpline information

### Embedded Responsible Gambling Messaging
Required on:
- All platform review pages
- All strategy/tips pages
- All bonus/promotion pages
- Homepage (at minimum in footer)

---

## Affiliate Disclosure

- The site must disclose affiliate relationships clearly
- Disclosure must be visible — not hidden in fine print
- Recommended placement: top of review pages AND in site footer
- Disclosure language: "Este site pode receber comissao por indicacoes feitas atraves de nossos links."

---

## Data Protection (LGPD)

- Privacy Policy must be published and kept current
- Cookie consent must be implemented
- User data collected via contact forms must be handled per LGPD requirements
- No selling of user data to third parties
- Data retention policies must be defined

---

## Content Accuracy Rules

- Platform information (bonuses, terms, licensing) must be verified before publishing
- All reviews must include a "last verified" date
- Outdated information must be updated or removed promptly
- No fabricated reviews, ratings, or testimonials

---

## High-Risk Content Areas

The following content types carry elevated compliance risk and require extra care:

| Content Type | Risk Level | Required Action |
|-------------|-----------|------------------|
| Bonus promotions | HIGH | Always include T&C disclaimer + wagering requirements |
| Strategy/tips | HIGH | Always include no-guarantee disclaimer |
| Platform reviews | MEDIUM | Verify accuracy; include responsible gambling note |
| Odds/predictions | HIGH | Frame as analysis only, never as guaranteed outcomes |
| News about regulation | MEDIUM | Verify sources; do not give legal advice |

---

## What to Avoid (Legal Red Flags)

- Claiming to be a licensed gambling operator
- Offering to place bets on behalf of users
- Collecting payment for betting tips or predictions
- Making specific financial return promises
- Targeting users in jurisdictions where online gambling is illegal
- Publishing content that could be seen as encouraging problem gambling
