## Purpose of FAQ Content

FAQ pages serve multiple strategic purposes:
1. **SEO** — Capture long-tail, question-based search queries (high volume in pt-BR)
2. **User Experience** — Provide quick answers to common questions
3. **Featured Snippets** — FAQ schema markup increases chances of appearing in Google's featured snippets and People Also Ask boxes
4. **Trust** — Demonstrates expertise and transparency

---

## FAQ Page Types

### 1. Site-Wide FAQ (/faq/)
A central FAQ hub covering the most common questions across all topics. Organized into sections by topic.

### 2. Section-Level FAQs
Each main section (Apostas Esportivas, Cassinos Online, Bonus, etc.) should have its own FAQ section, either:
- As a dedicated sub-page (e.g., /apostas-esportivas/faq/)
- As a FAQ section at the bottom of the hub page

### 3. Page-Level FAQs
Individual content pages (reviews, guides) should include 3–5 relevant FAQ items at the bottom of the page. These should:
- Address questions specific to that page's topic
- Use FAQ schema markup
- Link to more detailed content where relevant

## FAQ Content Guidelines

### Question Formatting
- Write questions as a user would actually type or speak them
- Use natural Brazilian Portuguese phrasing
- Start with question words: Como, O que, Qual, Quando, Por que, Posso, E legal
- Keep questions concise (under 15 words where possible)

### Answer Formatting
- Answers should be 40–150 words for most questions
- Start with a direct answer to the question (first sentence)
- Expand with supporting detail in subsequent sentences
- Include a relevant internal link where appropriate
- End with a WhatsApp CTA for questions about getting help or recommendations

### Example FAQ Structure

**Q: Como fazer um deposito via PIX em uma casa de apostas?**
A: Para depositar via PIX, acesse a secao de pagamentos da plataforma, selecione PIX como metodo de deposito, informe o valor desejado e escaneie o QR code gerado. O deposito e creditado em segundos. Veja nosso guia completo de deposito via PIX para instrucoes detalhadas.

## High-Priority FAQ Topics

### Apostas Esportivas
- Como funciona uma aposta esportiva?
- O que sao odds e como calcular ganhos?
- Qual e a diferenca entre apostas simples e multiplas?
- Como apostar ao vivo?
- E possivel ganhar dinheiro com apostas esportivas?

### Cassinos Online

- Como funciona o bonus de boas-vindas?
- O que sao rodadas gratis?

- Qual e o cassino online mais confiavel para brasileiros?

## FAQ Schema Markup

All FAQ sections must use proper FAQ schema markup (JSON-LD) to enable rich results in Google Search. This should be implemented on:
- The main /faq/ page
- All section-level FAQ pages
- All page-level FAQ sections

---

## FAQ Maintenance

- Review FAQ content quarterly
- Add new questions based on:
  - Google Search Console queries
  - WhatsApp questions received from community
  - Changes in regulation or platform terms
- Remove or update outdated answers promptly
- Track which FAQ items generate clicks via Search Console
