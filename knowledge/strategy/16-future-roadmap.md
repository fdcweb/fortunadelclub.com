# 16 — Future Roadmap

**Document:** Optional Future Improvements and Roadmap
**Knowledge Base:** FortunaDelClub Website Strategy
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-25

---

## Purpose

This document captures potential future improvements and strategic initiatives for FortunaDelClub. Items here are not currently active — they represent opportunities to pursue as the site grows and resources allow.

Items are organized by priority tier and estimated impact.

---

## Tier 1 — High Impact, Pursue When Ready

### 1.1 Dedicated Mobile App or PWA
**Description:** A Progressive Web App (PWA) version of the site that users can install on their home screen.
**Why:** Brazilian users are mobile-first. A PWA improves engagement, enables push notifications, and works offline.
**Considerations:** Requires development investment; ensure compliance with app store policies for gambling-adjacent content.

### 1.2 Email Newsletter
**Description:** A regular newsletter for community members with betting tips, platform updates, and responsible gambling reminders.
**Why:** Builds a direct communication channel independent of Google and WhatsApp.
**Considerations:** Requires LGPD-compliant opt-in; must include unsubscribe option; content must follow same compliance rules as website.

### 1.3 User-Generated Reviews
**Description:** Allow community members to submit their own platform reviews or ratings.
**Why:** Adds social proof, increases content freshness, builds community engagement.
**Considerations:** Requires moderation to prevent fake reviews; must not allow guaranteed-win claims; LGPD implications for user data.

### 1.4 Odds Comparison Tool
**Description:** A real-time or regularly updated odds comparison tool for major Brazilian sports events.
**Why:** High SEO value; high user utility; drives repeat visits.
**Considerations:** Requires data feed or manual updates; must include disclaimer that odds change and are for reference only.

---

## Tier 2 — Medium Impact, Plan for Future

### 2.1 Video Content
**Description:** YouTube channel or embedded video guides (how-to tutorials, platform walkthroughs).
**Why:** Video content ranks in Google and YouTube; appeals to Brazilian audience; builds trust through face/voice.
**Considerations:** Requires production resources; must follow same compliance rules as written content.

### 2.2 Podcast or Audio Content
**Description:** A Brazilian Portuguese podcast about sports betting, casino games, and responsible gambling.
**Why:** Growing podcast audience in Brazil; builds brand authority; community engagement.
**Considerations:** Requires consistent production schedule; compliance rules apply to audio content too.

### 2.3 Affiliate Program Diversification
**Description:** Expand beyond current affiliate partners to include more platforms, tools, and services.
**Why:** Reduces revenue concentration risk; increases monetization opportunities.
**Considerations:** Only partner with licensed, reputable platforms; maintain editorial independence.

### 2.4 Community Forum or Discussion Board
**Description:** An on-site forum or discussion area for community members.
**Why:** Increases time-on-site; builds community; generates user-created content for SEO.
**Considerations:** Requires active moderation; LGPD compliance for user accounts; responsible gambling rules apply to user posts.

---

## Tier 3 — Long-Term Vision

### 3.1 Multi-Language Expansion
**Description:** Expand content to Spanish for Latin American markets.
**Why:** Large addressable market; similar regulatory environment in some countries.
**Considerations:** Significant content investment; different regulatory requirements per country; separate domain or subdirectory strategy needed.

### 3.2 Proprietary Data and Research
**Description:** Publish original research on Brazilian betting habits, platform usage, and responsible gambling.
**Why:** Earns backlinks; builds authority; differentiates from competitors.
**Considerations:** Requires data collection methodology; survey tools; analysis resources.

### 3.3 Partnerships with Responsible Gambling Organizations
**Description:** Formal partnerships with Brazilian responsible gambling organizations.
**Why:** Strengthens E-E-A-T signals; demonstrates genuine commitment to responsible gambling; potential co-marketing.
**Considerations:** Requires outreach and relationship building.

### 3.4 Live Chat Support
**Description:** Add live chat (potentially WhatsApp-integrated) for real-time user support.
**Why:** Improves conversion; builds trust; reduces friction for users with questions.
**Considerations:** Requires staffing or chatbot investment; must not provide gambling advice or tips.

---

## Roadmap Review Process

This roadmap should be reviewed:
- **Quarterly** — Assess which Tier 1 items are ready to pursue
- **Annually** — Full roadmap review; promote items from lower tiers; retire items no longer relevant
- **When triggered** — New opportunities or threats may require roadmap updates outside the regular schedule

---

## How to Prioritize

When deciding which roadmap item to pursue next, consider:
1. **Impact on core KPIs** — Does it drive traffic, WhatsApp contacts, or affiliate revenue?
2. **Compliance risk** — Does it introduce new compliance complexity?
3. **Resource requirement** — What is the time and cost investment?
4. **Community demand** — Are users asking for this via WhatsApp or other channels?
