## Site Architecture Overview

FortunaDelClub follows a hub-and-spoke content architecture:
- **Hub pages** (pillar pages) cover broad topics and link to spoke pages
- **Spoke pages** cover specific sub-topics and link back to the hub
- **FAQ pages** support both hubs and spokes with long-tail keyword coverage

## URL Structure Rules

- All URLs in lowercase
- Use hyphens (-) not underscores (_)
- Keep URLs short and descriptive
- Include primary keyword in URL where natural
- Avoid dates in URLs for evergreen content
- Use trailing slash consistently

---

## Navigation Rules

- Main navigation: maximum 7 items
- All main sections accessible from homepage within 1 click
- All sub-pages accessible within 2 clicks from homepage
- Breadcrumbs on all sub-pages
- Footer navigation includes all legal pages
