**Knowledge Base:** FortunaDelClub Website Strategy

## Required Legal Pages

The following legal pages are mandatory for FortunaDelClub to operate compliantly:

| Page | URL | Priority |
|------|-----|----------|
| Privacy Policy | /politica-de-privacidade/ | CRITICAL |
| Terms of Use | /termos-de-uso/ | CRITICAL |
| Legal Disclaimer | /aviso-legal/ | CRITICAL |
| Cookie Policy | /politica-de-cookies/ | HIGH |
| Responsible Gambling | /jogo-responsavel/ | CRITICAL |

All legal pages must be:
- Linked from the site footer on every page
- Written in clear Brazilian Portuguese
- Reviewed and updated at least quarterly
- Dated with the last update date

---

## Privacy Policy (/politica-de-privacidade/)

### Must Cover
- What personal data is collected (name, email, IP, cookies, etc.)
- How data is collected (forms, cookies, analytics)
- Why data is collected (purpose of processing)
- How data is stored and protected
- Data retention periods
- User rights under LGPD (access, correction, deletion, portability)
- How to exercise LGPD rights (contact method)
- Third-party data sharing (analytics tools, affiliate networks)
- Cookie usage and consent
- Contact information for data-related inquiries

### LGPD Compliance Notes
- Must identify the Data Controller (the site operator)
- Must provide a contact channel for data subject requests
- Must be updated whenever data practices change
- Cookie consent banner must link to this page

---

## Terms of Use (/termos-de-uso/)

### Must Cover
- Nature of the site (informational only, not a gambling operator)
- User eligibility (18+ only, legal jurisdiction)
- Acceptable use of the site
- Intellectual property rights (content ownership)
- Limitation of liability (site is not responsible for third-party platform actions)
- No guarantee of accuracy (information may change)
- Affiliate relationship disclosure
- Governing law (Brazilian law)
- How to contact for terms-related questions

---

## Legal Disclaimer (/aviso-legal/)

### Must Cover
- FortunaDelClub is an informational/community site only
- The site does not operate gambling services
- Content is for informational purposes and does not constitute legal, financial, or gambling advice
- No guarantee of accuracy of third-party platform information
- Users are responsible for verifying platform terms before registering
- Gambling involves financial risk
- 18+ only
- Affiliate relationship disclosure
- Limitation of liability for losses incurred through use of recommended platforms

---

## Cookie Policy (/politica-de-cookies/)

### Must Cover
- What cookies are and how they work
- Types of cookies used (essential, analytics, marketing)
- Specific cookies in use (Google Analytics, affiliate tracking, etc.)
- How to manage or disable cookies
- Impact of disabling cookies on site functionality
- Link to Privacy Policy

### Cookie Consent Implementation
- A cookie consent banner must be displayed to new visitors
- Banner must offer Accept / Reject / Manage options
- Consent must be recorded
- Non-essential cookies must not load before consent is given

---

## Responsible Gambling Page (/jogo-responsavel/)

### Must Cover
- Definition of responsible gambling
- Signs of problem gambling (checklist format recommended)
- Self-assessment tools or links
- Self-exclusion options (how to self-exclude from major platforms)
- Support resources:
  - Jogadores Anonimos Brasil
  - CVV (Centro de Valorizacao da Vida) — crisis support
  - SENAD (Secretaria Nacional de Politicas sobre Drogas)
- How to set deposit limits on major platforms
- Encouragement to seek help without stigma

## Footer Link Requirements

The site footer must include links to ALL legal pages on every page of the site. Minimum footer legal section:

- Politica de Privacidade
- Termos de Uso
- Aviso Legal
- Politica de Cookies
- Jogo Responsavel
- Sobre Nos
- Contato
