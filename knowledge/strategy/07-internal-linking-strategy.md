# 07 — Internal Linking Strategy

**Document:** Internal Linking Guidelines and Page Connection Rules
**Knowledge Base:** FortunaDelClub Website Strategy
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-25

---

## Why Internal Linking Matters

Internal linking serves three critical functions for FortunaDelClub:
1. **SEO** — Distributes page authority (PageRank) across the site and helps Google understand site structure
2. **User Experience** — Guides users to relevant content and keeps them on the site longer
3. **Conversion** — Creates pathways that lead users toward WhatsApp CTAs and platform reviews

---

## Internal Linking Rules

### Minimum Requirements Per Page
- Every page must have **at least 2 internal links** to other pages on the site
- Every page must link **up** to its parent/hub page (except the homepage)
- Hub pages must link **down** to all their sub-pages
- Review pages must link to the relevant category hub (e.g., /avaliacoes/)

### Anchor Text Rules
- Use **descriptive, keyword-rich anchor text** — never "clique aqui" or "saiba mais" alone
- Anchor text should describe the destination page's topic
- Vary anchor text for links to the same page — avoid exact-match repetition
- Keep anchor text natural within the sentence

**Good examples:**
- "Veja nossa avaliacao completa do Bet365"
- "Aprenda como usar bonus de boas-vindas"
- "Confira nosso guia de apostas esportivas"

**Bad examples:**
- "Clique aqui"
- "Saiba mais"
- "Acesse o link"

---

## Hub-to-Spoke Linking

Every hub page must contain links to ALL its sub-pages, either:
- In the body content (contextual links)
- In a dedicated "Artigos Relacionados" or "Nesta Secao" section
- Or both

### Example: Apostas Esportivas Hub
The /apostas-esportivas/ page must link to:
- /apostas-esportivas/como-apostar/
- /apostas-esportivas/tipos-de-apostas/
- /apostas-esportivas/odds-e-cotacoes/
- /apostas-esportivas/futebol/
- (all other sub-pages in this section)

---

## Spoke-to-Hub Linking

Every sub-page must link back to its parent hub page. This is typically done via:
- Breadcrumb navigation (automatic)
- A contextual sentence in the introduction or conclusion
- A "Voltar para [Hub Name]" link at the bottom of the page

---

## Cross-Section Linking

Encourage relevant cross-links between sections:
- Strategy pages should link to relevant platform reviews
- Review pages should link to relevant bonus pages
- Bonus pages should link to how-to guides (e.g., how to use a bonus)
- All strategy/tips pages should link to the Jogo Responsavel page

### Cross-Linking Priority Map

| From Page Type | Should Link To |
|---------------|----------------|
| Platform review | Bonus page, comparison page, how-to guides |
| Strategy/tips | Jogo Responsavel, platform reviews |
| Bonus page | Platform reviews, how-to guides |
| How-to guide | Relevant platform reviews, FAQ |
| FAQ answer | Relevant full guide or review page |

---

## WhatsApp CTA Linking

Every page must include at least one link/button to the WhatsApp contact. This is treated as an internal conversion link and should be:
- Placed prominently (above the fold on mobile where possible)
- Repeated at the end of the page
- Styled as a button (not plain text) where design allows

---

## Links to Legal Pages

Legal pages (Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, Disclaimer) should be linked from:
- The site footer (on every page)
- The About page
- Any page that collects user data or makes legal claims

---

## External Linking Rules

- Link to licensed, reputable platforms only
- All affiliate/partner links must use proper tracking parameters
- External links should open in a new tab (target="_blank")
- Add rel="nofollow" or rel="sponsored" to affiliate links per Google guidelines
- Do not link to unlicensed or blacklisted gambling sites

---

## Internal Linking Audit

Perform an internal linking audit:
- **Quarterly** — Check for broken internal links
- **When adding new pages** — Update existing pages to link to the new page
- **When removing pages** — Update or redirect all links pointing to removed pages
