# 09 — Contact and Transparency

**Document:** Contact Information and Transparency Requirements
**Knowledge Base:** FortunaDelClub Website Strategy
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-25

---

## Transparency Philosophy

FortunaDelClub's credibility depends on being transparent about:
- Who operates the site
- How the site makes money
- What the site is and is not
- How users can get in touch

Transparency is not just a legal requirement — it is a core trust signal that differentiates FortunaDelClub from low-quality affiliate sites.

---

## Contact Page Requirements (/contato/)

The contact page must include:
- **Primary contact method:** WhatsApp (with clickable link/button)
- **Email address** (for formal inquiries)
- **Response time expectation** (e.g., "Respondemos em ate 24 horas")
- **Scope of support** — what the team can and cannot help with
- A note that FortunaDelClub is NOT a gambling operator and cannot assist with platform-specific account issues

### What FortunaDelClub Can Help With (via Contact)
- Questions about content on the site
- Recommendations for platforms based on user needs
- General information about betting and casinos
- Responsible gambling guidance and referrals

### What FortunaDelClub Cannot Help With
- Account issues on third-party platforms
- Disputes with betting/casino operators
- Legal or financial advice
- Placing bets on behalf of users

---

## WhatsApp as Primary Contact

WhatsApp is the primary contact and community channel. Rules:
- WhatsApp number/link must be visible on every page (header or floating button)
- The contact page must feature WhatsApp prominently
- WhatsApp CTA copy should be warm and inviting, not transactional
- Example CTA: "Fale com a gente no WhatsApp — estamos aqui para ajudar!"

---

## About Page Requirements (/sobre/)

The About page must include:
- **Who we are** — description of FortunaDelClub as a community and information platform
- **Our mission** — helping Brazilians make informed betting decisions
- **Editorial independence statement** — content is independently researched
- **Affiliate disclosure** — clear statement about how the site earns revenue
- **Team/community description** — humanize the brand (does not need to name individuals)
- **Contact information** — link to /contato/ and WhatsApp

---

## Affiliate Disclosure Requirements

Affiliate disclosure must appear:
1. On the About page (/sobre/)
2. In the site footer (every page)
3. At the top of individual review pages

**Standard disclosure text:**
"FortunaDelClub pode receber comissao quando voce se cadastra em plataformas atraves dos nossos links. Isso nao afeta nossas avaliacoes, que sao baseadas em pesquisa independente."

---

## Editorial Independence Statement

The site must maintain and communicate editorial independence:
- Reviews are based on independent research and testing
- Platforms cannot pay for positive reviews
- Ratings reflect genuine assessment of platform quality
- Negative aspects are reported honestly

**Standard editorial statement:**
"Nossas avaliacoes sao elaboradas de forma independente. Plataformas nao podem pagar por avaliacoes positivas. Nosso objetivo e fornecer informacoes precisas e uteis para nossa comunidade."

---

## Transparency Checklist

Every page should contribute to overall site transparency:

- [ ] Footer includes affiliate disclosure
- [ ] Footer includes "site informativo — nao somos operadores de jogos"
- [ ] Footer includes 18+ notice
- [ ] Contact page is accessible from every page (footer link)
- [ ] About page is accessible from every page (footer link)
- [ ] Review pages include "last verified" date
- [ ] Bonus pages include T&C disclaimer
