## Site Identity

**Website:** fortunadelclub.com

**Site Type:** Informational / Community / Affiliate-adjacent

---

## Mission Statement

FortunaDelClub exists to be the most trusted, informative, and community-driven resource for Brazilian sports betting and online casino enthusiasts. The site provides educational content, casino and sportsbook reviews, strategy guides, and community engagement — without operating as a gambling platform itself.

---

## Core Objectives

1. **Educate** — Help users understand how sports betting and online casinos work, including rules, odds, strategies, and responsible gambling.
2. **Inform** — Provide up-to-date, accurate reviews of licensed betting platforms available to Brazilian users.
3. **Build Community** — Foster a trusted community of bettors who share knowledge and experiences.
4. **Generate Leads** — Drive qualified traffic toward partner platforms via WhatsApp and affiliate links.
5. **Rank Organically** — Achieve strong Google rankings for high-intent Brazilian Portuguese keywords in the betting/casino niche.

---

## Target Audience

### Primary Audience
- Brazilian adults (18+) interested in sports betting and online casinos
- Beginner to intermediate bettors seeking guidance
- Users researching which platforms to use

### Secondary Audience
- Experienced bettors looking for strategy content and community
- Users seeking responsible gambling resources

### Audience Characteristics
- Mobile-first users (majority access via smartphone)
- WhatsApp-native communication preference
- Price-sensitive and bonus-motivated
- Skeptical of scams — trust and transparency are critical

---

## Business Model

- **Affiliate Revenue:** Commission from referred users to partner betting/casino platforms
- **Community Engagement:** WhatsApp group/channel as primary community hub
- **Lead Generation:** WhatsApp CTA as primary conversion mechanism
- **Content Authority:** Long-term SEO investment to reduce paid acquisition dependency

---

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Goal |
|--------|------|
| Organic search traffic | Primary growth channel |
| WhatsApp contact rate | Primary conversion KPI |
| Page rankings (pt-BR) | Top 10 for target keywords |
| Content freshness | Regular updates to maintain rankings |
| User trust signals | Reviews, disclaimers, transparency |

---

## Competitive Landscape

- Competing against large affiliate sites, news portals, and established review sites
- Differentiation through: community focus, WhatsApp accessibility, Brazilian-specific content, and trustworthy tone
- Must avoid appearing as a gambling operator — positioned strictly as informational/community

---

## Regulatory Context

- Brazilian gambling regulation is evolving (Lei 14.790/2023 and subsequent regulations)
- Site must comply with Brazilian advertising and consumer protection standards
- Must not target minors or vulnerable individuals
- Responsible gambling messaging is mandatory
