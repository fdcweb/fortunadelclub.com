# 15 — Risk Areas and Monitoring

**Document:** Risk Areas to Monitor and Warning Indicators
**Knowledge Base:** FortunaDelClub Website Strategy
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-25

---

## Overview

Operating in the Brazilian gambling information space carries specific risks. This document identifies the key risk areas and the warning signs to watch for, along with recommended responses.

---

## Risk Category 1: Regulatory Risk

### Description
Brazilian gambling regulation is actively evolving. New rules could affect what content is permissible, how affiliate relationships must be disclosed, or whether certain types of content are restricted.

### Warning Signs
- New legislation or regulatory announcements from the Ministry of Finance (Ministerio da Fazenda) or SPA (Secretaria de Premios e Apostas)
- CONAR rulings against gambling advertising
- News about enforcement actions against affiliate sites
- Changes to Lei 14.790/2023 or related regulations

### Monitoring Actions
- Follow SPA official communications
- Monitor Brazilian gambling industry news (e.g., iGaming Brasil)
- Set up Google Alerts for "regulamentacao apostas brasil" and related terms
- Review legal pages quarterly

### Response Protocol
- If new regulation affects site content: consult legal guidance and update content within 30 days
- If content becomes non-compliant: unpublish immediately, update, then republish

---

## Risk Category 2: Google Algorithm Risk

### Description
Google frequently updates its algorithms. Gambling/betting sites are in a "Your Money or Your Life" (YMYL) category and are subject to heightened scrutiny, especially around E-E-A-T.

### Warning Signs
- Sudden drop in organic traffic (20%+ in a week)
- Loss of rankings for previously stable keywords
- Google Search Console manual action notification
- Core algorithm update announced (check Google Search Central blog)

### Monitoring Actions
- Monitor Google Search Console weekly
- Track keyword rankings for top 20 target keywords
- Follow Google Search Central blog and SEO news sources
- Monitor Core Web Vitals scores monthly

### Response Protocol
- Traffic drop: Audit affected pages for E-E-A-T signals, content quality, and compliance
- Manual action: Address the specific issue immediately and submit reconsideration request
- Core update impact: Wait 2 weeks before making major changes; assess pattern of affected pages

## Risk Category 3: Content Accuracy Risk

### Description
Platform terms, bonuses, and features change frequently. Publishing outdated information damages user trust and can create compliance issues.

### Warning Signs
- User complaints via WhatsApp about incorrect information
- Platform changes its bonus terms or removes a feature
- A reviewed platform loses its Brazilian license
- Bonus amounts or wagering requirements change

### Monitoring Actions
- Check reviewed platforms monthly for major changes
- Monitor platform official communications and social media
- Track WhatsApp community questions for signs of outdated content
- Set up alerts for platform name + "mudancas" or "novo bonus"

### Response Protocol
- Minor change: Update within 2 weeks
- Major change (e.g., platform loses license): Update within 48 hours
- Incorrect bonus information: Update within 24 hours

---

## Risk Category 4: Compliance and Legal Risk

### Description
Publishing non-compliant content (e.g., guaranteed winning claims, targeting minors, missing disclaimers) could expose the site to legal action or regulatory penalties.

### Warning Signs
- Content published without required disclaimers
- User complaints about misleading information
- Affiliate partner requests to remove disclaimers or compliance language
- Legal notice or complaint received

### Monitoring Actions
- Compliance checklist review on every new page before publishing
- Quarterly audit of all pages for required disclaimers
- Review affiliate partner agreements for compliance requirements
- Monitor for user complaints via WhatsApp and contact form

### Response Protocol
- Missing disclaimer: Add immediately
- Misleading content: Correct or remove within 24 hours
- Legal notice received: Do not respond without legal guidance; seek legal advice immediately

---

## Risk Category 5: Platform/Affiliate Partner Risk

### Description
Affiliate partners may change terms, reduce commissions, or exit the Brazilian market. Over-reliance on a single partner creates revenue risk.

### Warning Signs
- Affiliate partner announces exit from Brazilian market
- Commission structure changes significantly
- Partner platform receives negative press or regulatory action
- Affiliate program terms change unfavorably

### Monitoring Actions
- Monitor affiliate partner communications
- Diversify affiliate partnerships — do not rely on a single platform
- Track affiliate revenue by partner monthly
- Monitor partner platform reputation and regulatory status

### Response Protocol
- Partner exits market: Update/remove review; find replacement partner
- Commission reduction: Reassess content priority for that partner
- Partner receives regulatory action: Add prominent warning to review; consider removing recommendation

---

## Risk Category 6: Reputation Risk

### Description
The site's reputation depends on trust. Any perception of bias, inaccuracy, or unethical behavior can damage the brand.

### Warning Signs
- Negative community feedback in WhatsApp group
- Social media criticism of site content
- Accusations of paid reviews or biased content
- Community members reporting scams linked to site recommendations

### Monitoring Actions
- Monitor WhatsApp community sentiment
- Set up Google Alerts for "FortunaDelClub" brand mentions
- Respond promptly to community concerns
- Maintain editorial independence — never accept payment for positive reviews

### Response Protocol
- Negative feedback: Acknowledge, investigate, and respond transparently
- Accusation of bias: Review the content in question; update if warranted; publish correction if needed
- Scam report: Investigate immediately; warn community if confirmed; update or remove related content
