# 04 — Tone and Language

**Document:** Tone, Language Guidelines, and Vocabulary Rules
**Knowledge Base:** FortunaDelClub Website Strategy
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-25

---

## Language Standard

- **Primary language:** Brazilian Portuguese (pt-BR)
- **All public-facing content** must be written in pt-BR
- **Internal documentation** may be in English or Portuguese
- Avoid European Portuguese spellings and expressions
- Use Brazilian colloquialisms where appropriate, but maintain professionalism

---

## Tone of Voice

### Overall Tone
**Warm, knowledgeable, and trustworthy** — like advice from a well-informed friend who is part of the same community.

### Tone by Content Type

| Content Type | Tone |
|-------------|------|
| Platform reviews | Balanced, analytical, honest |
| How-to guides | Clear, patient, step-by-step |
| Strategy content | Informative, cautious, realistic |
| Community/WhatsApp CTAs | Warm, inviting, peer-to-peer |
| Legal/compliance content | Formal, precise, clear |
| Responsible gambling | Empathetic, non-judgmental, supportive |

---

## Language Rules

### Always DO
- Write in active voice where possible
- Use short sentences and paragraphs for mobile readability
- Address the reader directly ("voce" not "o usuario")
- Use Brazilian payment terms: PIX, Boleto Bancario
- Reference Brazilian football (Brasileirao, Copa do Brasil, Selecao)
- Use local platform names as Brazilians know them

### Never DO
- Use European Portuguese terms (e.g., "telemovel" instead of "celular")
- Use overly formal or bureaucratic language
- Use English terms when a natural Portuguese equivalent exists
- Use slang that could be offensive or exclusionary
- Write in a way that sounds machine-translated

## Responsible Language Rules

> **CRITICAL:** Never use language that implies guaranteed outcomes.

### Banned Phrases
- "Ganhe dinheiro garantido"
- "Lucro certo"
- "Estrategia infalivel"
- "100% de chance de ganhar"
- Any variation of "guaranteed winnings" or "sure bet" presented as fact

### Required Framing for Strategy Content
Always frame strategy content with language such as:
- "Esta estrategia pode ajudar a gerenciar seu bankroll..."
- "Lembre-se: apostas envolvem risco..."
- "Nenhuma estrategia garante lucros..."

---

## Readability Standards

- **Target reading level:** Accessible to adults with secondary education
- **Sentence length:** Aim for 15–20 words average
- **Paragraph length:** 2–4 sentences maximum for body content
- **Use of headers:** Every 200–300 words, use an H2 or H3
- **Lists and tables:** Preferred over dense paragraphs for comparisons and steps
- **Bold text:** Use to highlight key terms and critical information

---

## SEO Language Considerations

- Include target keyword naturally in the first 100 words
- Use keyword variations and synonyms throughout
- Avoid keyword stuffing — content must read naturally
- Meta descriptions should be written in conversational pt-BR
- Title tags should be compelling and include the primary keyword
