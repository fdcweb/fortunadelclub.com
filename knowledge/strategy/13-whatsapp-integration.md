## WhatsApp as the Core Conversion Channel

WhatsApp is the primary conversion mechanism for FortunaDelClub. It serves as:
- **Community hub** — where the FortunaDelClub community lives
- **Lead generation** — users who contact via WhatsApp are warm leads
- **Support channel** — answering questions and providing recommendations
- **Trust signal** — a real, accessible contact method builds credibility

Every page on the site should have a clear path to WhatsApp contact.

---

## WhatsApp CTA Placement Rules

### Required Placements (Every Page)
1. **Floating button** — Fixed position, visible on scroll, bottom-right corner on mobile
2. **End of page CTA** — Before the footer, a clear WhatsApp CTA section

### Recommended Additional Placements
3. **Above the fold** — On high-intent pages (reviews, bonus pages), include a WhatsApp CTA in the hero/intro section
4. **Mid-content** — On long pages (1,500+ words), include a WhatsApp CTA mid-way through
5. **After key sections** — After a platform rating or bonus summary, prompt users to ask questions via WhatsApp

## WhatsApp CTA Copy Guidelines

### Tone
- Warm, inviting, community-oriented
- Not pushy or sales-heavy
- Peer-to-peer language

### CTA Copy Examples (Use and Adapt)

**General CTA:**
"Tem duvidas? Fale com a nossa comunidade no WhatsApp!"

**Review page CTA:**
"Quer saber mais sobre o [Platform Name]? Pergunte no nosso WhatsApp!"

**Bonus page CTA:**
"Precisa de ajuda para entender os termos do bonus? Estamos no WhatsApp!"

**Strategy page CTA:**
"Quer discutir estrategias com outros apostadores? Entre no nosso grupo do WhatsApp!"

**Homepage CTA:**
"Junte-se a comunidade FortunaDelClub no WhatsApp — dicas, analises e muito mais!"

### CTA Copy Rules
- Always use "WhatsApp" (not "Zap" or "WA") in formal CTAs
- Include a clear action verb: "Fale", "Entre", "Pergunte", "Junte-se"
- Keep CTAs under 15 words where possible
- Never promise guaranteed tips or profits in CTA copy

## WhatsApp Button Design

### Floating Button
- Use official WhatsApp green color (#25D366)
- Include WhatsApp logo/icon
- Visible on all pages, all screen sizes
- Should not obscure important content
- Accessible (proper ARIA labels for screen readers)

### Inline CTA Buttons
- Green button with white text
- Clear label: "Falar no WhatsApp" or "Entrar no Grupo"
- Minimum size: 44px height for mobile tap targets
- Opens WhatsApp with a pre-filled message where possible

---

## Pre-filled WhatsApp Messages

Use pre-filled message links to reduce friction. Format:
https://wa.me/[NUMBER]?text=[URL-ENCODED-MESSAGE]

### Example Pre-filled Messages by Page Type

**From a review page:**
"Ola! Vi a avaliacao do [Platform] no FortunaDelClub e tenho uma duvida."

**From a bonus page:**
"Ola! Tenho uma pergunta sobre o bonus de [Platform]."

**From a general page:**
"Ola! Vim do site FortunaDelClub e gostaria de mais informacoes."

---

## WhatsApp Group vs. Direct Contact

### If using a WhatsApp Group/Channel:
- Link should direct to the group/channel join link
- Set clear community rules (no spam, no scams, respectful discussion)
- Moderate the group to maintain quality
- Pin important messages (site updates, responsible gambling reminders)

### If using Direct WhatsApp Contact:
- Set up a WhatsApp Business account
- Configure auto-reply for off-hours
- Respond within 24 hours maximum
- Keep a FAQ document to answer common questions quickly

---

## WhatsApp and Compliance

- Never use WhatsApp to send unsolicited messages
- Do not collect WhatsApp numbers without consent
- Do not use WhatsApp to promote specific bets or "sure tips"
- Community messages must follow the same responsible gambling standards as the website
- Do not share affiliate links in WhatsApp without proper disclosure

---

## Tracking WhatsApp Conversions

- Use UTM parameters on WhatsApp links where possible to track traffic source
- Monitor WhatsApp contact volume as a KPI
- Track which pages generate the most WhatsApp contacts (use Google Analytics events)
- Use this data to optimize CTA placement and copy
