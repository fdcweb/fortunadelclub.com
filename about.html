About FortunaDelClub

Our Story

FortunaDelClub is an independent lottery assistance and information service operating in India, focused on officially conducted Goa State Lottery and Kerala State Lottery schemes.

We have been operational for over 7 years, assisting 1,600+ participants across various official lottery draws. Our focus has consistently been on transparency, responsible participation, and extended customer support.

We provide clear, compliant guidance based on official sources and encourage responsible participation in all interactions.

What We Do (And What We Don't)

We Provide

  • Guidance on official draw schedules and timings
  • Clarification of prize structures per official notifications
  • Assistance with participation coordination per official procedures
  • Support with result verification post official announcements
  • General guidance on prize claim processes

We Do Not

  • Sell lottery tickets or conduct lottery draws
  • Influence draw outcomes or guarantee winnings
  • Operate as a government lottery authority
  • Replace official government sources for results or announcements

Responsible Participation

FortunaDelClub actively encourages responsible participation in lottery activities. Participants are advised to:

  • Understand applicable rules before participating
  • Rely on official government announcements for results
  • Participate within their personal means
  • Verify all details through official government sources

Legal & Operational Position

FortunaDelClub is an independent lottery assistance service and is not affiliated with any state lottery department. All lottery draws are conducted exclusively by respective state authorities.

Participation assistance is provided in accordance with applicable local regulations.
