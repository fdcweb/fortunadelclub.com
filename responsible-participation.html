Responsible Participation

FortunaDelClub's commitment to safe, informed, and compliant lottery participation assistance.

Effective Date: 1 January 2024  |  Last Updated: 2 May 2026

"Lottery participation is a matter of personal choice.
FortunaDelClub exists to inform — not to encourage or pressure."

1. Our Commitment

FortunaDelClub is committed to providing lottery assistance and information in a responsible, transparent, and non-coercive manner. We believe that informed participants make better decisions, and that our role is to assist — not to promote, pressure, or incentivise participation.

This policy outlines the principles we follow and the expectations we have of persons who use our services.

2. Lottery Participation Is Not Investment

FortunaDelClub firmly states:

  • Lottery participation is a form of entertainment and chance — not a financial strategy or investment
  • There is no guaranteed return from any lottery draw
  • Past participation outcomes do not indicate future results
  • No strategy, system, or assistance can predict or influence lottery outcomes
  • All draw results are determined solely by the respective State Lottery Departments through officially notified draw processes

We do not make financial return claims. We do not encourage participation as a means of income or wealth generation.

3. Participate Within Your Means

FortunaDelClub strongly advises all participants:

  • Set a budget — only participate with amounts you are fully comfortable losing
  • Do not borrow funds to participate in any lottery draw
  • Do not prioritise lottery participation over essential personal or family expenses
  • Treat participation as an occasional, discretionary activity — not a routine financial commitment
  • Stop if it stops being enjoyable — participation should never cause stress or hardship

4. Age and Legal Eligibility

  • Lottery participation is restricted to persons of legal age as defined by applicable law in their jurisdiction
  • In India, state lottery participation is generally restricted to persons aged 18 years and above
  • FortunaDelClub does not provide assistance to persons who are minors
  • Participants are solely responsible for confirming their eligibility to participate under applicable law

5. Legal Compliance

  • Goa State Lottery and Kerala State Lottery are government-authorised schemes conducted under respective state regulations
  • Participants must ensure that lottery participation is lawful in their jurisdiction before engaging
  • FortunaDelClub does not provide assistance in jurisdictions where lottery participation is prohibited by law
  • Participants are fully responsible for understanding and complying with the laws applicable to them

6. Information Accuracy

  • All information shared by FortunaDelClub is based on publicly available, officially notified lottery information
  • We do not share speculative, predictive, or unofficial information about draw outcomes
  • Draw schedules, prize structures, and ticket formats may change per official government notifications — participants must verify through official sources
  • FortunaDelClub is not responsible for decisions made solely on information published on this Website without independent verification

7. No Pressure, No Guarantees

FortunaDelClub explicitly commits that:

  • We will never pressure any person to participate in a lottery draw
  • We will never guarantee a winning outcome
  • We will never misrepresent participation odds or prize probabilities
  • We will never use misleading language to encourage participation (e.g. "guaranteed win", "insider tips", "best strategy")
  • Participation is always the participant's free and informed choice

8. Signs of Problem Gambling

If you or someone you know is experiencing any of the following, please seek appropriate help:

  • Spending more than you can afford on lottery tickets
  • Chasing losses by participating repeatedly after not winning
  • Neglecting family, work, or personal responsibilities due to lottery participation
  • Feeling anxious, stressed, or distressed about lottery outcomes
  • Borrowing money specifically to participate in draws
  • Being unable to stop despite wanting to

FortunaDelClub encourages anyone experiencing such issues to seek support from qualified organisations. In India, you may contact:

Support Resources

  • iCall (Tata Institute of Social Sciences): icallhelpline.org | 9152987821
  • Vandrevala Foundation Helpline: 1860-2662-345 (24×7)
  • NIMHANS (National Institute of Mental Health): nimhans.ac.in
