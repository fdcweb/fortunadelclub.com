Understanding the number and series structure on Goa State Lottery tickets helps participants accurately verify results against official announcements.

Ticket Number Structure

Goa State Lottery tickets carry a structured identification format comprising a series letter followed by a 6-digit number.

Format Components

  • Series Letter: A single or double letter prefix assigned per draw batch (e.g., A, B, AA)
  • 6-Digit Number: A unique number within the series (e.g., 123456)
  • Example format: A 123456
  • Series and numbers are pre-printed and assigned by official convention

Purpose

  • Enables systematic result verification
  • Allows prize checking against officially declared winning numbers
  • Different series may carry different prize eligibility per official notification

How to Verify Your Ticket

After a draw is conducted, winners are identified by matching the ticket series and number against the officially declared results.

Verification Steps

  • Note your ticket series letter and 6-digit number
  • Check the officially declared results via the Goa State Lottery website
  • Match your number against the prize-winning numbers for your draw date
  • If your number matches, follow the official prize claim process
  • All final verification must be done through official government sources

Official Result Sources

  • statelotteries.goa.gov.in
  • myrajshree.com – Today's Results
